Our mission

Our mission is to transition the power to retail investors by bringing accessibility to all facets of investing. And when we say we want to democratize investing for all, we mean it.

Why we're here

  • The problem.

    Retail investors account for roughly 20% of stock-market activity on average. Hedge funds, investment banks, and other institutional investors control the majority of the market. We want to narrow that gap.

  • The Idea.

    Create an investing app for the retail investor (people) that is completely conflict-free from Wall Street. We do this in three ways:

    1) Mudani does not accept any institutional investors on its platform, only retail.

    2) Mudani does not accept any backing or funding from the “big boys” on Wall St.

    3) Mudani does not take any cut in the ”Payment for order flow” (PFOF).

  • The Vision.

    Our founders started at the very root of the problem – on Wall Street. Witnessing the disparity and obscurity first hand at these institutional powerhouses, they decided to create a team that catered to the retail investor and not just the wealthy.

  • The Solution.

    After two years of development, finding the right team with a common belief, and partnering with best-in-class technology, Mudani was born. Our goal remains simple. Cater to every type of retail investor, no matter their bank account balance, know-how, or net worth. To be an all-in-one fintech platform that makes investing easy, educational and fun.

    To be transparent, trusting, and true to our word.
