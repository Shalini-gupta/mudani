Margin & Options Trading

With options, you can be bullish on stocks you believe in and bearish on the ones you don’t. Although risky, options trading can be rewarding.

Options trading with Mudani is simple. You can either buy a call or buy a put. That way, the most you can lose is your principal. We want to ensure we’re keeping our members safe from speculative trading and infinite losses.
